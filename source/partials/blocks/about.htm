About us

EFROM DIGITAL is a Republic of Seychelles registered business company with office in EU. EFROM DIGITAL’s aim is to create the world’s best blockchain platform DAEFROM

DAEFROM is developed to be a stable Proof of Work platform based on Ethereum and own features. Born in the crypto spring of 2022, DAEFROM is a new blockchain that contained premine small value of coins for support Research and development (R&D) department.

Primary focus is improving the core features of the ETH fork blockchain.
